Delegation is per-axis. A group owner grants and revokes membership of their own group and approves its join requests. They cannot change anyone's clearance — that stays with workspace admins — and owning a group confers no read access to its documents. Ownership and membership are separate grants; most owners hold both, but the system records them independently so neither implies the other.
